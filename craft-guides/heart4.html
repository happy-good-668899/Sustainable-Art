Origami Hearts Dandelion

Step 1

Cut a 1 inch by 1 inch square.

Step 2

Fold the square into quarters by joining opposite corners and folding the square in half two times.

Step 3

Fold down the top corner of the square to the center of the square.

Step 4

Fold up the bottom corner to the top line of your origami.

Step 5

Fold 1/2 of the bottom edge to the center line of the heart.

Step 6

Glue down this flap.

Step 7

Repeat steps 5 and 6 on the other side and get your finished heart.

Step 8

Arrange hearts as shown and repeat the heart-creation process over and over.
