Heart Envelope from Grocery Bags

Step 1

Cut 1.5 inch by 1.5 inch squares from grocery bag paper. Fold square into quarters, creating diagonals from corner to corner of your square.

Step 2

Fold the bottom corner of the square to the middle.

Step 3

Fold the top corner of the square to the middle as well.

Step 4

Turn your paper 90 degrees and fold the bottom edge just shy of the middle, creating a gap between the fold and the other folded triangles.

Step 5

Fold up the bottom edge to just higher than the middle of your paper and glue.

Step 6

Let glue dry to get your envelope.

Step 7

Create many hearts using varying square sizes. Refer to the whimsical origami hearts for instructions on how to make a heart

Click for heart steps

Step 9

Arrange in pattern as shown to get final result.
